Physics-First Approach

No black-box embeddings. Every feature is directly extracted from the transit shape physics: depth, duration, ingress fraction, secondary eclipses, and stellar priors.

Rigorous Leakage Prevention

We identified and eliminated NASA vetting confidence proxies (koi_prad, fpflag_*) that caused 98% accuracy via data leakage. V4 achieves honest 73% accuracy.

Centroid Motion Detection

The #1 most important feature (17.4% importance). If the center of light shifts during a dip, the source is a background star — this is exactly how NASA identifies blends.
